# Creator & influencer program

How NeuroShot partners with course authors, AIGC mentors, and bloggers — the
deal structures we can offer, the economics guardrails, and the tooling that
makes a deal live the same day it's agreed.

## The tooling (shipped)

- **Partner codes** — `t.me/<bot>?start=c_<code>`. Each code carries *its own
  negotiated terms*: a lifetime percent of every purchase by users it brings,
  and a welcome gift of 🔫 patrons for those users. First-touch attribution,
  immutable, exclusive with friend referrals (no double payouts).
- **`/partner` dashboard** — the creator sees, per code: joined → paying →
  patrons earned, plus their terms and link. No spreadsheets, no "trust me".
- **Admin `/partner_add <code> <tg_id> <percent> <join_bonus> [title]`** —
  a deal goes live in one message.
- Payouts land as 🔫 patrons in the creator's own account (they can spend or
  we settle off-platform at an agreed rate — commercial choice, not code).

## Economics guardrails

Purchased patrons run 4.5–6× margin over AI cost (see `pricing.md`). A creator
share comes out of revenue, so:

| Creator share | Worst-case margin (9⭐ pack) | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| 10% (organic referral default) | ~4.0× | always fine |
| 20% | ~3.6× | **recommended ceiling for year-1 deals** |
| 25% | ~3.4× | OK if their audience buys mid/large packs |
| >30% | <3.2× | only for strategic white-label deals |

The invited-user join bonus is marketing cost: keep it 5–20 🔫 (one to a few
image generations — enough to feel the product, not enough to farm).

## Offer menu (for negotiating with a course author / mentor)

Ordered from lowest to highest mutual commitment. Bundle 2–3; keep 4 as the
escalation path, not the opener.

1. **Affiliate+** — personal code, **20–25% lifetime** of every purchase from
   their audience (vs 10% public referral), custom welcome gift (+10 🔫) branded
   with their name in the greeting. Zero risk for us, instant to launch.
2. **Course integration** — NeuroShot becomes the hands-on tool inside their
   course: students practice on real generations without buying their own API
   keys. Their cohort gets a bigger welcome pack; the author gets wholesale
   patron packs (−40–50%) to distribute as course material. We get distribution
   into exactly the audience that converts; they get a turnkey practice tool.
3. **Signature presets** — their named style presets in the bot & app («Пресеты
   от …»), with a share of generations run through them. Their brand lives
   inside the product — the strongest retention hook for their audience.
   *(Preset attribution is backlog — see below.)*
4. **White-label** — their own branded bot skin on our infra, same backend and
   ledger, ~50/50 on their traffic. Only for a partner bringing serious volume
   and committing to promotion cadence.

**What to ask for in return** (make the deal two-sided): a dedicated module in
the course using NeuroShot, N posts/stories per month, student case studies we
can reuse, and their feedback channel (course students are a live QA panel).

## Backlog that strengthens creator deals

- **Signature preset packs** with per-generation attribution & payout (offer 3).
- **Promo codes** (one-off bonus drops for launches/streams) — distinct from
  partner codes: no revenue share, just a gift + attribution window.
- **Wholesale pack invoicing** for course bundles (Stars invoices to one buyer,
  patrons distributed to a list).
- **Partner web dashboard** in the Mini App (same numbers as `/partner`, plus
  charts) — part of the premium web redesign.
- **Card paywalls** beyond Stars (Crypto → Kaspi → YooKassa, roadmap §F) — many
  course audiences pay by card, not Stars.
